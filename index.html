About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Graduate School of Advanced Imaging Science, Multimedia & Film (GSAIM) at Chung-Ang University, advised by Prof. Hak Gu Kim.

My research integrates vision, language, and audio through multimodal learning, aiming to build systems that understand and interact with humans. I work on speech-driven 3D facial animation, where phonetic and articulatory cues guide audio-visual generation, and on multimodal large language models (MLLMs) that extend language understanding beyond text. I am now extending this line toward socially-aware systems that reason about human interaction across modalities.

Research interests: Multimodal LLM (MLLM) · Cross-modal Learning · Social Artificial Intelligence
